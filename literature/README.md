# Literature

Background reading on hazardous waste regulation, enforcement, and the effects of
both. These papers are not used by the code. They are here to orient a reader and
to place the data in the questions researchers have asked of it, several of which
turn on the federal and state enforcement themes in the
[state authorization brief](../docs/institutional_briefs/04_state_authorization.md) and
the [compliance and enforcement brief](../docs/institutional_briefs/03_compliance_and_enforcement.md).

| File | What it is |
|------|------------|
| The Effect of Punishment on Firm Compliance with Hazardous Waste Regulations, Sarah L. Stafford, 2002.pdf | Whether enforcement and penalties change firm compliance behavior under RCRA. |
| Assessing the Effectiveness of State Regulation and Enforcement of Hazardous Waste, Sarah L. Stafford, 2003.pdf | How well state-run hazardous waste programs regulate and enforce. |
| Determinants of Environmental Law Violation Fines Against Petroleum Refineries - Race, Ethnicity, Income, and Aggregation Effects, Michael J. Lynch, Paul B. Stretesky, Ronald G. Burns, 2004.pdf | What drives the size of fines against refineries, including community demographics. |
| An Empirical Study of US Environmental Federalism - RCRA Enforcement from 1998 to 2011, 2018.pdf | State versus federal enforcement patterns under RCRA over fourteen years. |
| Does the Criminal Enforcement of Federal Environmental Law Deter Environmental Crime? The Case of the U.S. RCRA, Joshua Ozymy, Melissa L. Jarrell, 2021.pdf | Whether criminal prosecutions under RCRA deter environmental crime. |
| Hazardous Waste and Home Values - An Analysis of Treatment and Disposal Sites in the United States, Dennis Guignet, Christoph Nolte, 2024.pdf | How proximity to treatment and disposal sites affects residential property values. |
| Layers of Injustice - A Distributional Assessment of Toxic Chemical Facilities, Releases, and Cleanups, Sarah Brodin, Dennis Guignet, 2024.pdf | Who lives near toxic facilities, releases, and cleanups. |
