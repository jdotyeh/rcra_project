Same as federal definitions. | No state-specific waste subject to RCRA manifest and reporting requirements. | [Utah Department of Environmental Quality, Division of Waste Management and Radiation Control](https://deq.utah.gov/waste-management-and-radiation-control/hazardous-waste-management-program) [Encamp](https://encamp.com/rcra-hazardous-waste-compliance/utah/) |
| Vermont Uses RCRAInfo, Paper Copy accepted | LQGs and TSDFs are required to submit Biennial Reports through RCRAInfo by March 1 of every even-numbered year, and paper reporting is allowed only with an approved waiver. SQGs and VSQGs are exempt from reporting but must register annually and pay fees by April 30. SQGs re-notify the state every 4 years by submitting form 8700-12 by September 1 (2021, 2025). Same as federal definitions. | Vermont maintains state waste codes (VT01 through VT22) covering wastes such as PCBs over 50 ppm, pesticide waste, ethylene glycol, and PFOA and PFOS at or above 20 ppt. | [Vermont Department of Environmental Conservation](https://dec.vermont.gov/waste-management/hazardous/which-regulations/biennial-hazardous-waste-report-information) [Encamp](https://encamp.com/rcra-hazardous-waste-compliance/vermont/) |
