# Institutional Briefs

This folder collects what a person needs to know about the hazardous waste
regulatory program in order to read the data correctly. The data in this project
are administrative records produced by that program, so a column only means what
the rule behind it says it means. These briefs record those rules and, at the end
of each one, spell out what they imply for the tables and panels built here.

Start with the [institutional overview](00_overview.md). It explains the program
as a whole and how its parts map onto the RCRAInfo modules and the ECHO extracts.
The topic briefs then go one level deeper.

- [00_overview.md](00_overview.md) is the program as a whole and the map from
  program function to data source.
- [01_biennial_report.md](01_biennial_report.md) is the Biennial Report,
  who reports and when, and the waste tonnage figures.
- [02_generators_and_handlers.md](02_generators_and_handlers.md) is the regulated
  universe, the generator size categories, notification, and TSDFs.
- [03_compliance_and_enforcement.md](03_compliance_and_enforcement.md) is
  inspections, violations, significant noncompliance, and enforcement actions.
- [04_state_authorization.md](04_state_authorization.md) is the division of labor
  between EPA and the states and why it makes records differ across state lines.
- [05_corrective_action.md](05_corrective_action.md) is cleanup of releases at
  regulated facilities.
- [06_permitting_and_closure.md](06_permitting_and_closure.md) is permits,
  closure, and post-closure.
- [07_financial_assurance.md](07_financial_assurance.md) is the requirement that
  facilities prove they can pay for closure and cleanup.
- [08_waste_import_export.md](08_waste_import_export.md) is the cross-border
  movement of hazardous waste.
- [09_facility_identifiers.md](09_facility_identifiers.md) is the several
  identifiers a facility carries and how they are linked here.
- [10_epa_forms.md](10_epa_forms.md) is the three EPA forms behind the records,
  the notification, the Hazardous Waste Report, and the Part A application, and
  the values EPA computes from the Biennial Report filings.
- [11_manifests_and_shipment_tracking.md](11_manifests_and_shipment_tracking.md)
  is the manifest that follows each shipment of waste and the electronic system
  that now collects it.
- [12_waste_codes_and_management_methods.md](12_waste_codes_and_management_methods.md)
  is the code vocabulary for what a waste is, where it came from, its form, and
  what was done with it.
- [13_universal_waste_used_oil_and_recycling.md](13_universal_waste_used_oil_and_recycling.md)
  is the lighter regimes inside the program and the facility roles they create.
- [14_regulatory_citations.md](14_regulatory_citations.md) is how a violation
  points at the requirement it rests on and who owns the rule cited.

These briefs are written from public EPA program materials and from the
state-by-state reporting reference in `resources/table.md`. They are background,
not a legal reference, and they are meant to be revised as the project grows.
